# FAQ

Frequently asked questions about ImpactMojo.

---

## General

**Is ImpactMojo free?**
Yes. All courses, games, labs, handouts, ImpactLex, case studies, and the Dataverse are free. Premium tiers (starting at ₹399/month) add export tools, certificates, and coaching.

**Do I need to create an account?**
No. You can access all free content without logging in. An account unlocks progress tracking, bookmarks, notes, learning streaks, and community features.

**What devices does it work on?**
Any device with a modern web browser — desktop, laptop, tablet, or mobile. The site is a Progressive Web App and can be installed for offline access.

**Does it work offline?**
Partially. Installed as a PWA, cached content is available offline. Account features and interactive games require an internet connection.

## Content

**How many courses are there?**
48 total — 9 flagship courses (deep, multi-module programmes) and 39 foundational courses (shorter, skill-focused).

**What languages are supported?**
English, Hindi, Tamil, Bengali, Telugu, and Marathi. The roadmap includes professional translations (not machine-generated) of primary courses.

**What are Course Decks?**
23 foundational courses are available as visual 101 slide decks featuring Indian folk art illustrations, suitable for classroom projection.

**What is ImpactLex?**
A searchable, browsable glossary of 500+ development terms, available as a PWA for offline use.

## Workshops

**Can I use ImpactMojo content in my training?**
Yes. Educational content is licensed CC BY-NC-ND 4.0 — you can share and use it for non-commercial purposes with attribution.

**How do I plan a workshop?**
See [Workshops & Facilitation](workshops-and-facilitation.md) for step-by-step planning guides and sample agendas for 1-day, 3-day, and 5-day formats.

**Can I track participant progress?**
Yes, with an Organisation tier subscription. Team dashboards track member progress, engagement, and certificate completion.

## Premium & Payments

**What do premium tiers include?**

| Tier | Price | Key Features |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Explorer | Free | All core content |
| Practitioner | ₹399/month | RQ Builder Pro, certificates, community |
| Professional | ₹999/month | All 7 premium tools, priority coaching |
| Organization | ₹1,499/user/month | Team dashboards, custom pathways, branded certificates |

**How do I pay?**
Manual UPI payment (no gateway fees). International customers can pay via PayPal or Wise by contacting support.

**Is there a refund policy?**
Yes — 7-day refund guarantee on all subscriptions.
