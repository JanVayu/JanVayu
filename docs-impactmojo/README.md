# ImpactMojo Documentation

**ImpactMojo** is a free learning platform for people working in international development, social impact, and public policy across South Asia. It provides courses, games, labs, handouts, case studies, and data tools — all without paywalls or login requirements.

> **Live platform:** [www.impactmojo.in](https://www.impactmojo.in)
> **GitHub:** [github.com/JanVayu/ImpactMojo](https://github.com/JanVayu/ImpactMojo)

---

## What is ImpactMojo?

Development practitioners, researchers, NGO workers, and students across South Asia need accessible, high-quality education in MEL, research methods, economics, and policy. ImpactMojo delivers this through 48 courses, 16 games, 19 labs, 400+ handouts, 200 case studies, 500+ curated papers, and 247 datasets — all free at the point of use.

## Guiding Principles

- **Free at the point of use** — core content requires no login or payment
- **Practitioner-first** — designed for people doing the work, not just studying it
- **South Asia-focused** — case studies, data, and context from the region
- **Accessible** — works on slow connections, older devices, and offline via PWA
- **Open** — code is MIT-licensed; educational content is CC BY-NC-ND 4.0
