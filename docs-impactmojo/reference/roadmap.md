# Roadmap

ImpactMojo's development roadmap, organised by timeline.

---

## Active Development (Q1 2026)

### Vernacular Content
Professional translations (not machine-generated) of primary courses into Hindi, Tamil, Bengali, and Marathi. Starting with flagship courses and most-used handouts.

### Cohort-Based Learning
Scheduled group learning with enrollment windows, deadlines, and discussion threads. Organisation admins can create and manage cohorts from the team dashboard.

### Notification System
In-app alerts and email digests via Resend covering:
- Course updates and new content
- Engagement and streak reminders
- Cohort deadlines and assignments
- Community activity

---

- Enhanced analytics dashboard for learners and organisations
