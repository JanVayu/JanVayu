# Policy & Budget Tracker

JanVayu tracks India's major air quality policies against their stated targets and actual outcomes, with a focus on the National Clean Air Programme (NCAP) and GRAP.

---

## National Clean Air Programme (NCAP)

NCAP was launched in January 2019 with a target of **20–30% reduction in PM2.5 and PM10** in 132 Non-Attainment Cities by 2024 (later revised to 2026).

### What JanVayu Tracks

| Indicator | Source |
|-----------|--------|
| NCAP budget allocated vs. disbursed | Union Budget documents, MoEFCC |
| City Action Plans — submitted vs. approved | PRANA Portal (CPCB) |
| Non-Attainment Cities — target vs. actual PM2.5 | CPCB Annual Reports |
| 15th Finance Commission clean air grants | Ministry of Finance |

### PRANA Portal

The PRANA (Portal for Regulation of Air-pollution in Non-Attainment cities) portal at [prana.cpcb.gov.in](https://prana.cpcb.gov.in) is the official tracking mechanism. JanVayu links directly to PRANA data for each city.

---

## GRAP (Graded Response Action Plan)

GRAP is an emergency action protocol for Delhi-NCR that triggers restrictions based on AQI levels.

| Stage | AQI Trigger | Key Measures |
|-------|------------|--------------|
| Stage I | 201–300 (Poor) | Ban on coal/wood burning, dust control |
| Stage II | 301–400 (Very Poor) | Diesel generator restrictions, BS III/IV vehicle limits in NCR |
| Stage III | 401–450 (Severe) | Construction bans, school hybrid mode |
| Stage IV | 450+ (Severe+) | Truck entry bans, odd-even vehicle scheme consideration |

JanVayu archives GRAP stage history with dates, so you can correlate emergency restrictions with AQI readings.

## Budget Tracker

The Budget Tracker shows fund utilisation rates for:

- **NCAP grants** — ₹4,400 crore allocated (2019–2024)
- **15th Finance Commission** — ₹12,139 crore for cities with >1 million population over 5 years
- **State-level disbursements** — how much each state received and spent

Low utilisation rates are a key accountability metric. JanVayu flags cities where funds remain unspent.

## Supreme Court & NGT Orders

The Legal Framework section archives:

- Supreme Court of India orders on air quality (especially in *M.C. Mehta v. Union of India*)
- National Green Tribunal (NGT) directions
- Commission for Air Quality Management (CAQM) orders for NCR

All orders are linked to [Indian Kanoon](https://indiankanoon.org) for full text.

---

## RTI Templates

The platform provides pre-drafted Right to Information (RTI) templates for:

- Requesting NCAP fund utilisation data from state pollution control boards
- Requesting GRAP compliance reports from CAQM
- Requesting source apportionment study results from CPCB
- Requesting industrial emission monitoring data from state PCBs

Templates are available in English and Hindi, formatted for submission under the RTI Act 2005.
